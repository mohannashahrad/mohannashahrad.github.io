A unified dynamics framework and particle solver focusing on modelling fluids (liquids and gases) and fluid-solid coupling based on Macklin et. al's Unified Particle Physics for Real-Time Applications.
" collection: portfolio --- This project implements a particle-based physics simulation inspired by Macklin et al.'s "Unified Particle Physics for Real-Time Application." It introduces a unified dynamics framework and particle solver specifically designed for modeling fluids (liquids and gases) and fluid-solid coupling. By utilizing particles connected by constraints, the solver allows for the unified treatment of contact and collisions, enabling efficient simulations. [Code].
